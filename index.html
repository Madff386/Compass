Surviving Your First Week At R.H.S

Things To Remember

  1. Always do your homework
  2. Never be late for class
  3. Make friends
  4. Get good grades
  5. Behave yourself
  6. Always be prepared for class
  7. Go to lots of clubs
  8. Earn form points
  9. Be yourself
  10. Have fun

Things To do if you need help

  1. Tell a teacher
  2. Tell your friend
  3. Tell the principal or assistant principals
  4. Tell another trusted adult
  5. Tell your parent(s)

Darcy H

I’m Darcy H, I go to Richmond
high school, in 2019 I was in
year 7 and I was in the class 7c.
High school is great you don’t
get very much homework and
you can make lots of friends.
Some other bits of advice is go
to lots of clubs to get form points.
We lost the form points competition
in 2019 I hope you win.

In my first week I got the code to
my locker and got my time table.
Some more advice: don’t lose it.
It takes a while to settle in but
after that it is really fun. I really
enjoyed my first week at Richmond
high school. I hope you enjoy
yours too.
